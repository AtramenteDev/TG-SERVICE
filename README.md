## 🔎 Overview

**TG VoIP Service** is a Telegram bot that allows users to browse and purchase virtual VoIP phone numbers from multiple countries directly within Telegram. Once a number is purchased, the bot handles SMS verification code retrieval with a single tap » no external apps or websites needed.

Upon starting the bot, users are greeted with their name, current balance and access to all available features through a clean inline menu.

---

## ⚙️ Features

💰 **Balance System** » View your current balance in € and recharge it at any time

☎️ **Virtual VoIP Numbers** » Browse a paginated list of countries with flag, name and price » select and purchase instantly

🔄 **SMS Verification** » Request your verification code directly from the bot with a single tap » the button updates automatically once the code is received

🔗 **Referral System** » Share your personal referral link and earn automatically from every recharge made by invited users

👤 **Personal Profile** » View your Telegram ID, current balance, total purchases and total amount spent

🌍 **Multi-Language** » Full support for 4 languages » all texts adapt to the user's chosen language

🆘 **Live Support** » Send a message directly to the admin and receive a reply inside the bot

---

## 🔄 How It Works

**Purchasing a number:**

» Press ☎️ VoIP from the main menu

» Browse the paginated country list with ◀️ ▶️

» Select a country » review availability and price

» Confirm the purchase » balance is deducted and the number is delivered instantly

» Press 🔄 to request the SMS verification code » the button turns ✅ once received

---

## 💳 Payment Methods

**➕ Recharge your balance via:**

» 💳 **Stripe** » enter the amount and receive a secure payment link

» 🪙 **Crypto** » via NowPayments » enter the amount and receive a crypto invoice

» 🅿️ **PayPal** » manual payment via [@Atramente](https://t.me/Atramente) » minimum recharge: **5€**

## 🔗 Referral System

» Access your personal referral link from the 🔗 Referral section

» Every time a user you invited recharges their balance, you automatically earn **2% of the recharge amount**
